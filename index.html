About Us

PharmaChat is an AI-based project developed by Pharmalite in collaboration with Medicofact. We are immensely grateful to our investors, who have been the backbone of this project. Our primary goal is to address the queries and concerns of pharmacy and medical students, helping them understand complex concepts and providing answers to their questions.

  • PharmaChat: Your AI-powered assistant for pharmacy and medical queries, providing accurate answers and clarifying concepts in real-time.
  • PharmaChat can provide instant responses, answer medical queries, and offer guidance on medications, symptoms, and general healthcare information.
  • Simplifying Healthcare Education: Leveraging artificial intelligence to streamline learning, PharmaChat helps students and professionals understand complex topics, from drug synthesis to disease management.

Note: PharmaChat is an AI-powered assistant providing information on pharmacy and medicine. While we aim for accuracy, occasional errors are possible. Verify critical information independently and consult healthcare professionals for personalized advice.
